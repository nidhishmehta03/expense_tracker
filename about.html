📖 About Finance Tracker

Welcome to the Personal Finance Dashboard! This simple web app helps you track your income and expenses effortlessly, keeping you on top of your financial health.

Features:

  • Add Transactions: Easily input income and expenses with descriptions.
  • Real-time Summary: View total income, expenses, and balance at a glance.
  • Transaction History: Keep a list of all your transactions.
  • Future Updates: Charts and detailed reports coming soon!

Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for a lightweight, no-install experience. Start managing your finances today!
